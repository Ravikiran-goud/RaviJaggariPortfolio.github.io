KAI AI Onboarding Design

In this project, I designed a seamless onboarding experience for KAI AI, integrating UI/UX principles with AI-driven design elements. The goal was to create a user-friendly and engaging onboarding process that fits within the existing KAI platform.

Project Overview

This project involved understanding design principles, Figma, and Generative AI to create a high-fidelity design that aligns with the user needs and enhances the product experience.

Design Process

I started by researching UX/UI design principles and the capabilities of the Figma tool. This informed my design decisions, ensuring that the onboarding flow was intuitive and aligned with the KAI AI platform's design language.
